Moodada 链问
Example of a Large-Scale Decentralized Collaboration

Blockchain Revolution is all about collaboration. And, Moodada is an example of how Large-Scale Decentralized Collaboration could be done using MOAC MicroChain Technology.


Stores of Time Sensitive Information

Be it a realtime match score of a Champions League game, or the best place in Stadium to wait for Steven Curry's signature, you can always find an answer here in Moodada. Also, if you know the answer to the question, you can earn yourself a chicken dinner as well.

Real Decentralized

  • A complete blockchain wallet: supports MOAC coin, ERC token, and MicroChain tokens.
  • No fees for end-user: no token is needed to start to use the DApp.
  • No Backend: all information is on-chain and transparent.
  • No Centralized Admin: arbitration and management is done through the community with token incentive.
